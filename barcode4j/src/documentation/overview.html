Barcode4J is a flexible generator for barcodes written in Java.

Barcode4J supports a variety of barcode symbologies such as Interleaved 2 of 5, Code 39, Code128, Codabar, EAN-8/13, UPC-E/A, POSTNET, Royal Mail Customer Barcode, PDF417 and DataMatrix.

Website:http://barcode4j.sourceforge.net
